## Conclusion
## Conclusion When pharmacological management of ADHD is indicated in an adult, psychostimulants, including methylphenidate and amphetamines, are first line. Atomoxetine is an option if psychostimulants cannot be used or tolerated. Pharmacological management for adults with ADHD requires a nuanced, patient-centred approach grounded in comprehensive assessment and ongoing monitoring for treatment response and adverse effects.
